# TechBridge Nigeria — BI Sales Analytics 2024

## Project Overview
- Dataset: TechBridge Nigeria 2024 sales data
- Tool: Microsoft Excel
- Orders: 413 | Regions: 4 | Channels: 3 | Sales Reps: 10

## Data Cleaning
- Removed duplicates
- Fixed date formats
- Flagged missing Sales Reps and Regions
- Corrected text errors in Product and Channel columns
- Filled missing Channel entries
- Added placeholders for blank Discount fields
- Calculated Discount Amount, Revenue and Net Revenue
- Validated product categories using XLOOKUP

## Analysis
- Revenue by Sales Rep
- Revenue by Region
- Revenue by Channel
- Quantity and Average Unit Price by Product
- Order Status breakdown
- Monthly Revenue Trend
